The AI coding landscape in early 2026 is defined by rapid model releases, deepening adoption, real cost pressures, and unresolved debates about where this is all heading.

This section surveys the terrain as of February 2026. It is not a tool comparison (see the [Tool Landscape](/references/tool-landscape/)) or a how-to guide (see [Start Here](/start-here/)). Instead, it captures the broader context that shapes every practitioner decision: which models exist, how far adoption has reached, what the economics look like, and what questions remain genuinely open.
